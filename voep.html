Design Project

VOEP

Overview

Developed VOEP, a gamified extracurricular platform that boosts student engagement by integrating self-awareness, leadership, and communication skill development into everyday campus activities. ​

Grounded in extensive research—including focus groups, surveys, and persona studies—to uncover student pain points and optimize club/event participation. ​

Incorporated dynamic gamification elements such as achievement badges, leaderboards, virtual rewards, and interactive quizzes to encourage healthy competition and sustained involvement. ​

Designed a seamless, multi-device user experience with intuitive interfaces and real-time notifications (including smartwatch integration) to keep students informed and actively connected with campus activities.
