Design Project

NOM NOM

Overview

NomNom is an innovative gamified learning app that transforms healthy eating into an engaging adventure for children through interactive gameplay, kids collect nutritious items to empower their avatar while unhealthy choices visibly drain energy and performance. ​

By blending playful UX design with behavioral insights, NomNom motivates young users to adopt healthier eating habits both in-game and in real life.

An app / Platform that transforms healthy eating into a fun, interactive adventure where children collect nutritious items to boost their avatar’s energy and abilities. ​

Provides immediate visual feedback on food choices, reinforcing the benefits of healthy eating and the drawbacks of junk food through engaging gameplay. ​

Combines user-centric research with cutting-edge UX design tools to create a gamified learning experience that promotes lasting, real-life healthy habits. ​
