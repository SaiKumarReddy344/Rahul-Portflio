Industry Connect Project

🔄 Taledge

Overview

​Crafted a sequential, panel-based layout that tells Taledge’s brand story through illustrated characters and speech bubbles—guiding users step-by-step through key benefits in a fun, engaging way while preserving information hierarchy.

Applied Taledge’s signature colors, typography, and visual assets throughout website redesign across both light and dark themes, ensuring seamless brand recognition and a cohesive comic aesthetic that adapts elegantly to user preferences.

Embedded clear calls-to-action at pivotal moments in the narrative “arc,” turning story beats into conversion opportunities.

Structured each comic panel and accompanying text to reflow cleanly across screen sizes—maintaining legibility, sufficient contrast, and accessible interaction targets.
